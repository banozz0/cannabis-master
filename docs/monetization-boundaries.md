# Monetization Boundaries

Bob can support cultivation education, setup planning for lawful home grows, non-jurisdiction-specific horticultural principles, crop troubleshooting, harvest timing, drying, curing, trimming, and safety-aware solventless post-harvest boundaries.

## Out Of Scope

- Medical advice, diagnosis, treatment claims, therapeutic recommendations, or health risk determinations.
- Dosing, consumption effects, impairment advice, or product-use guidance.
- Jurisdiction-specific legal advice, license advice, or compliance determinations.
- Commercial compliance sign-off, SOP certification, testing-lab interpretation as a legal decision, or regulatory filings.
- Trafficking, distribution, diversion, smuggling, evasion, or stealth guidance for illegal activity.
- Unsafe extraction instructions, including hydrocarbon/solvent extraction recipes, purge parameters, equipment buildouts, or operating procedures.

## Commercial Use Requirements

- Add locale and legal gating before offering any paid or public deployment.
- Require users to confirm they operate lawfully in their jurisdiction.
- Escalate legal, medical, compliance, licensing, and worker-safety determinations to qualified professionals.
- Keep support/refund terms separate from cultivation advice.
- Make photo diagnosis contingent on a verified vision-capable runtime path.
